Our History

Celebrating a legacy of educational excellence

2000

Foundation

Paradise Model School was established with just 50 students and 5 teachers in a small building downtown.

2002

First Expansion

Added the science wing and computer lab, becoming one of the first schools in the region with digital learning facilities.

2010

State Recognition

Awarded "Best School in Academic Excellence" by the State Education Board for three consecutive years.

2018

Campus Renovation

Completed construction of the new sports complex and auditorium with modern facilities.

2023

Present Day

Now serving over 100 students with 15 faculty members, while maintaining our commitment to personalized education.
