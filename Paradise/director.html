Director's Message

Leadership with vision and dedication

Mr Abolade Ezekiel Kayode

Director, Paradise Model School

"Education is not the filling of a pail, but the lighting of a fire. At Paradise, we strive to ignite that spark of curiosity in every student."

With over 25 years of experience in educational leadership, Mr Abolade has guided Paradise Model School to become one of the most respected institutions in the region. His philosophy centers on creating an environment where academic rigor meets creative exploration.

A Message to Our School Community

Dear Students, Parents, and Well-wishers,

It is my privilege to welcome you to Paradise Model School, where we believe in nurturing not just scholars, but well-rounded individuals equipped to make meaningful contributions to society.

In our rapidly evolving world, education must extend beyond textbooks. That's why we emphasize:

  • Critical thinking over rote memorization
  • Character development alongside academic achievement
  • Technology integration with human values

I invite you to partner with us in this transformative educational experience.
